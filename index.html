About Us

A Light Company is a forward-thinking, customer-centric business dedicated to providing high-quality lighting solutions that not only brighten your spaces but also contribute to a sustainable future. We are committed to reducing electronic waste, offering eco-friendly options, and providing exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead the lighting industry with innovative designs, sustainable practices, and unparalleled customer satisfaction. We aim to deliver top-quality lighting solutions that are both environmentally friendly and cost-effective, ensuring our customers always receive the best value for their money.

Product Features

  • Variable Brightness: Our lights come with adjustable brightness settings, allowing you to create the perfect ambiance in any room.
  • Lifetime Warranty: Every light bulb from A Light Company comes with a 2-year standard warranty. After the second year, if your bulb fails, simply return it to the retailer to receive a discount on your next purchase.
  • Discount on Replacement: Customers who return a fused bulb after the 2-year standard warranty period are eligible for a 10-20% discount on their next purchase from A Light Company.
  • Competitive Pricing: We offer high-quality lights at market-competitive prices.
  • Sustainable Design: Our lights are designed with sustainability in mind.
  • Wide Range of Products: We offer a diverse range of lighting solutions to meet every need.
  • Innovative Designs: Our design team is constantly working to bring you the latest in lighting trends.
